Shelved — Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

Data Collection

Shelved does NOT collect, store, or transmit any personal data.

  • No accounts required. You don't sign up. You don't log in.
  • No servers. All data stays on your device in local storage.
  • No analytics. We don't track how you use the app.
  • No ads. We don't serve advertisements or use ad networks.
  • No third-party sharing. We have nothing to share.

What stays on your phone

  • Items you log (name, price, status)
  • Your streak data
  • Your settings and savings goals

All of this is in local SQLite and AsyncStorage. Delete the app and it's gone.

RevenueCat

We use RevenueCat for one-time purchase validation only. RevenueCat processes an anonymous purchase receipt. No personal data is sent.
